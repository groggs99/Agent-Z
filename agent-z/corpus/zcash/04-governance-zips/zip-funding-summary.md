## ZIP-1014: Establishing a Dev Fund for ECC, ZF, and Major Grants

The foundational funding ZIP. Established the Zcash Dev Fund after the first halving (November 2020):
- 20% of mining rewards allocated to development
- Split: ECC (7%), Zcash Foundation (5%), Major Grants (8%)
- Major Grants (now ZCG) receives the largest single allocation
- Established the community-elected grants committee structure
- Duration: through the second halving (approximately November 2024, then extended)

## ZIP-1015: Block Subsidy Allocation for Non-Direct Development Funding

Continuation and refinement of the funding mechanism. Extended the dev fund concept with adjustments to allocation and governance.

## ZIP-1016: Establishing a Dev Fund for ECC, ZF, ZCG, and Zcash Community Grants 2

The current funding framework:
- Continues 8% of mining rewards to ZCG
- Extends through the third halving (November 2028)
- Introduced lockbox mechanism for potential future community-directed allocation
- Reflects the evolution from "Major Grants" to "Zcash Community Grants"

## Key facts for grant applicants

- ZCG funding comes from mining rewards, not a fixed treasury — inflow is continuous but ZEC-denominated.
- The funding stream has a known end date (November 2028) unless extended by future governance.
- The ZCG committee is community-elected, not appointed by ECC or ZF.
- This independence is important: ZCG is not beholden to any single organisation.
