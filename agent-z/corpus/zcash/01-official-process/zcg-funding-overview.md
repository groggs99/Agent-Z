# ZCG Funding Overview

Source URL: https://zcashcommunitygrants.org/
Collected date: 2026-05-27
Source type: official ZCG homepage
Status: primary source. Financial figures will change; verify against dashboard.

## Funding mechanism

ZIPs 1014, 1015, and 1016 were approved by the Zcash community, allocating 8% of mining rewards to provide sustained major grant funding for the ecosystem through the third halving in November 2028.

## Current treasury (as of 2026-05-27)

- Approximately 100,000 ZEC
- Approximately $11M USD at $110 ZEC/USD
- Dashboard: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1FQ28rDCyRW0TiNxrm3rgD8ai2KGUsXAjPieQmI1kKKg/edit?usp=sharing

## Key financial context

- Funding is denominated in ZEC but grant amounts are specified in USD.
- ZEC price volatility means the real purchasing power of the treasury fluctuates.
- The funding stream continues through the third halving (November 2028).
- Larger grants require more diligence and milestones.
- Grants over $50,000 USD require KYC.

## Previously funded project examples (from ZCG homepage)

- Ywallet (Hanh) — performant light wallet for mobile and desktop
- ZecHub — open-source education hub and contributor onboarding
- Free2z — web3 platform powered by Zcash for content creators
- Shielded Support for Ledger Hardware Wallets (Hanh) — first shielded Zcash on hardware wallet
- Zcash-Avalanche Elastic Subnet Bridge (red·dev) — cross-chain bridge
- Shielded Assets on Zcash (QEDIT) — ZSAs for DeFi on Zcash
- Zcash Media — educational platform about Zcash, privacy, and related issues

## Agent Z usage rules

- Always check the dashboard for current figures before making financial claims.
- When helping size a grant, reference the treasury as context: a $50k grant is ~0.5% of current treasury; a $500k grant is ~5%.
- Flag that ZEC price volatility affects payout value between milestones.
- First-time applicants should generally start smaller (under $50k) to avoid KYC friction and build track record.
