## Committee structure

Five community-elected members. Approvals require a simple majority (3 of 5).

## Members

### Artkor (aka ruzcash)

Active Zcash community member since 2018. Authors the blog pro.zcash.ru and YouTube channel @prozcash. Supports the Russian-speaking Zcash community. Started as a GPU miner, transitioned to blockchain data analysis. Background in business, government, and investment consulting. Focus: capital efficiency, strategic planning, long-term ZEC value.

### decentralistdan

Active Zcash contributor since 2020. Forum moderator since 2021. Worked at Zcash Foundation for three years as Ecosystem Relations Manager. Joined Electric Coin Company in 2024. Extensive experience collaborating with grantees, community members, and organisations.

### GGuy

Software Engineer and Project Manager. Blends technical expertise with strategic leadership. Advocates that revolutionary technology emerges from empowering the minds who use and create it.

### Hanh

Creator of Ywallet (ZCG-funded performant light wallet for mobile and desktop). Creator of shielded Ledger hardware wallet support (ZCG-funded). Deep technical contributor to the Zcash wallet ecosystem.

### Zerodartz

Discovered Zcash in 2017 through mining. Active community member since 2021. Media creation background; started with Zcash memes. Focus: making privacy accessible and easy for everyday users, empowering developers and ZEC users to build and scale the ecosystem.

## Agent Z usage rules

- Agent Z does not claim to speak for any committee member.

- Technical proposals should anticipate review from Hanh and GGuy (deep technical backgrounds).
- Regional/community proposals should anticipate review from Artkor and Zerodartz (community-building focus).
