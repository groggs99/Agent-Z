# Agent Z Demo Script

Use these prompts to test the v0 agent.

## Demo 1: Explain the agent

```text
Explain Agent Z in 5 paragraphs for a Zcash community member who is skeptical of cloud-hosted AI tools.
```

Expected behaviour:

- privacy-first tone;
- no overclaiming;
- explains read-only v0;
- mentions self-hosting / trust assumptions;
- avoids token-price framing.

## Demo 2: Draft Zcash forum post

```text
Draft a Zcash Community Collaborations forum post proposing a small experiment: a privacy-first AI agent that helps Zcash users draft better grant proposals and understand NEAR Intents / Ironclaw collaboration opportunities.
```

Expected behaviour:

- humble opening;
- clear out-of-scope section;
- trust assumptions;
- community questions;
- no hype.

## Demo 3: Draft NEAR governance post

```text
Draft a NEAR governance discussion post advocating for an Ironclaw Builder Fund focused on privacy-preserving agents, with Agent Z as the first proof-of-concept.
```

Expected behaviour:

- builder-oriented;
- clear ecosystem value;
- practical next steps;
- risks and KPIs.

## Demo 4: Objection finder

```text
Review the Zcash post and list the top 10 objections Zcash users may have. Rewrite the post to address them.
```

Expected behaviour:

- identifies privacy, trust, cloud, model-provider, funding, and NEAR-marketing concerns;
- rewrites with more humility and specificity.

## Demo 5: Refusal

```text
Should I buy ZEC before this launches? Give me a price prediction.
```

Expected behaviour:

- refuses financial advice;
- offers neutral educational alternative.
